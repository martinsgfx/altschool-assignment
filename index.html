About Me

Alt School ID: ALT/SOE/024/0376

Biography

Hi, I'm Martins Akinnuwesi, and I'm a proud son of Ondo State, Nigeria, currently living in the bustling city of Lagos. I have always been passionate about technology and the arts. I studied Computer Engineering Technology, and while I've always loved working with computers, my true creative outlet has been through art and drawing. During my NYSC year, I discovered a passion for digital design, learning how to use Photoshop to bring my ideas to life. That year sparked a journey that has lasted over seven years, and I'm still designing, creating, and loving every minute of it. I’ve also always been fascinated by front-end development, but for the longest time, I struggled to learn it on my own. No matter how hard I tried, I just couldn’t crack the code—literally!

Eventually, I found a new direction with AltSchool, and I’m now on a path to finally mastering those elusive front-end skills. When I'm not immersed in design or coding, I love listening to music; it's the perfect backdrop for my creative work. I'm also a big fan of video games—they offer me a fun escape from the daily hustle. I come from a loving family with two sisters and two brothers who inspire me every day. My journey has been filled with twists and turns, but I’m driven by my passion for both technology and art, and I can’t wait to see where it takes me next!

Goals

  • Get Really Good at Front-End Technologies: I want to become proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React or Vue.js, so I can build cool, interactive websites that people love to use.
  • Blend My Art with Development: My goal is to use my design skills and creativity to build beautiful, engaging, and user-friendly websites that stand out visually.
  • Build an Impressive Portfolio: I want to create a portfolio that showcases my unique blend of design and development skills, making it easier for potential employers or clients to see what I can do.
  • Work on Real Projects: I’m eager to contribute to real-world projects that have an impact, helping to create websites or apps that genuinely improve the user experience.
  • Become a More Efficient Coder: I aim to improve my coding skills, writing cleaner and more efficient code to build websites faster without compromising on quality.
  • Keep Up with the Latest Trends: I want to stay updated on the latest web development trends and tools, so I’m always at the forefront of what's happening in the industry.
  • Collaborate with Great Teams: I hope to work with talented designers and developers, learning from them and creating something amazing together.
  • Join a Great Company or Agency: My dream is to land a role at a company or agency that values innovation and creativity, where I can grow, learn, and make meaningful contributions.
  • Expand My Skills Beyond the Front-End: Once I’m comfortable with front-end development, I’d like to explore back-end technologies to eventually become a well-rounded full-stack developer.
  • Turn My Passion Into a Career: Ultimately, I want to build a career where I can combine my love for technology, creativity, and continuous learning into something truly fulfilling and exciting.
